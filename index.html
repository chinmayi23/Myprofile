"Your Adventure" Travel Campaign

For a luxury chocolate brand, I wrote compelling packaging copy that elevated the brand’s premium image. The packaging included poetic descriptions of the ingredients, the origins of the cocoa beans, and the artisanal process used to create each chocolate bar.
